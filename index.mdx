Welcome to the Promptrun.ai documentation, your guide to building, managing, and deploying prompts across environments and models.

Promptrun.ai is a prompt execution stack designed for teams and developers shipping LLM-powered applications, AI Agents, and more. Whether you're managing a few key prompts or scaling across multiple environments, this documentation will help you:

- Store and version prompts across dev/staging/prod and custom environments.
- Run prompts across multiple models via a unified API.
- Collaborate with your team on prompt changes and reviews.
- Build AI Agents and more.
